Case definitions:

Influenza-like-illness (ECDC case definition)

Sudden onset of symptoms 
AND at least one of the following four systemic symptoms:
Fever and/or chills
Feeling tired or exhausted
Headache
Muscle pain
AND at least one of the following three respiratory symptoms: 
Cough
Sore throat
Shortness of breath (dyspnoea)


Common cold

AT LEAST ONE of the following symptoms:
Runny or blocked nose
Sneezing
Sore throat
Cough
AND NOT Fever OR Low grade fever (if measured)


Allergy/hay fever

No fever
AND AT LEAST ONE of the following symptoms:
Sneezing
Runny or blocked nose
Watery, bloodshot eyes


Gastroenteritis or ‘gastric flu’

No respiratory symptoms, i.e. NOT: watery or bloodshot eyes; sneezing; runny or blocked nose; sore throat; cough; coloured sputum; chest pain; 
AT LEAST TWO of the following symptoms:
Diarrhoea
Nausea 
Vomiting
Loss of appetite
Stomach ache

Other symptoms

Note: IF AT LEAST ONE SYMPTOM CHECKED, BUT THE COMBINATION OF SYMPTOMS IS SUCH THAT THEY ARE NOT DEFINED IN ANY OF THE ABOVE CATEGORIES, THEN DEFINE AS “Other symptoms”.
